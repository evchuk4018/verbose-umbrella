# Integrated Team Strategy Handout

One-sheet, dual-sided summary connecting Scouting, Build Team, and Programming Team impact.

## Scouting Team
• Last year we entered most matches blind and paid for it in strategy and alliance outcomes.
• This year, our scouting app lets admins monitor coverage, assign matches, and review pre-scouting.
• We collect autonomous path data, average route trends, and scoring heatmaps for every team.
• Team lists come from TBA and live EPA comes from Statbotics, improving real-time match prep and alliance selection.
• Pit scouting captures desirable robot qualities; match notes are condensed into digestible insights by our fine-tuned LLM.

## Build Team
• Build decisions are driven by observed scoring locations, autonomous constraints, and repeatable design patterns from scouting data.
• Pit scouting highlights desirable mechanisms and reliability traits, giving Build concrete targets before redesign cycles.
• Build and Programming align on hardware capabilities needed for defensive autos, precision paths, and robust control.

## Programming Team
• Programming uses scouting route data to create defensive autonomous routines that block specific opponent paths.
• Heatmaps show where opponents can score, enabling efficient defense with less wasted movement.
• Strategy now translates directly into autonomous and teleop behavior, producing consistent on-field execution.

## How Integration Changed Results
Scouting informs Programming and Build with objective data; Build gives Programming reliable hardware to execute strategic plans; Programming turns scouting intelligence into actionable autonomous and defensive behavior. Together, this is a night-and-day improvement in match prep, alliance selection, and student development.
